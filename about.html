company profile

Fast Delivery Services is a leading global provider of comprehensive logistics and transportation solutions. Established in 1975 as an express operator, the company rapidly evolved into a global brand recognized for its customized services and innovative multi-product offering. In January 1999, Fast Delivery Services went public and traded its shares on the NASDAQ stock exchange.

After five years of successful trading, Fast Delivery Services returned to private ownership in February 2002 and continued to expand and excel as a privately owned company, establishing global alliances and gaining stronger brand recognition. Today, Fast Delivery Service employs more than 25,000 people in over 554 locations across 50 countries, and has a strong alliance network providing worldwide presence. The range of services offered by Fast Delivery Service includes international and domestic express delivery, freight forwarding, logistics and warehousing, records and information Management solutions, e-business solutions, and online shopping services.
