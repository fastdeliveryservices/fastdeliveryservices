INTERNATIONAL LOGISTICS & TRANSPORTATION

We have been in business since the mid 80’s during the “major airfreight development period.” We have been members of the International Air Transport Association (IATA) since 1991 and are also members of the Household Goods Forwarders Association of America, Inc., and (HHGFAA).

We have over 25 years of experience with a staff that takes pride in offering the best customer service to our clients. Our vast experience allows you to count on us to handle all of the endless details, which are part of international relocations. Our specialty of moving people instead of hard cargo makes us especially sensitive to the needs of our clients. We understand the stresses involved with moving and know all of the problems that arise when a transferee needs their personal effects “yesterday.” Give us an opportunity to work with you. We offer very competitive rates and feel that our experience will exceed your expectations. Give us the opportunity to quote some of your business. Fast Delivery Services

Our scheduled and timed delivery service provides fast, economic delivery of customers’ consignments, from important documents and parcel delivery to large consignments of product. Our door-to-door service is complete with proof of delivery for peace of mind for your business efficiency. At WGE, our timed delivery service is truly flexible and reliable, which allows our customers the option to choose a time that suits them. Alternatively, our scheduled delivery times are pre-09:00hrs, pre-10:30hrs, and pre-14:00hrs.

End-to-end solution available

When it comes to End to End Freight Delivery, Fast Delivery Service have the expertise to ensure your cargo reaches any destination in the world and we currently provide international freight shipping services in over 150 Countries. Whether it's freight by road, air freight, or sea freight shipping, our dedicated team offers a complete and immediate response with an exceptional, personal service - 24 hours a day. Our commitment has enabled us to become a leading freight company, with diverse experience, such as the transportation of live seafood and the supply of cruise liners during worldwide voyages. We are also experienced in providing our comprehensive freight delivery services to areas with unique challenges, such as Iraq or Afghanistan.

Safety & Compliance

Fast Delivery Services are qualified to handle dangerous goods throughout the freight delivery process. With many years of experience in the transportation and delivery of hazardous items, WGE can offer practical advice and solutions for both packing and transportation of your dangerous goods.
